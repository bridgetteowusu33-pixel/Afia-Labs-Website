Thank you to every early user of MemeScanr who downloaded the app, sent feedback, reported bugs, left reviews, and told other people about it. You helped turn this from an idea in my head into something real in the world.

Thank you to the builders online who shared honestly about what it takes to make and ship things. Your words helped me more than you probably know. I hope this book does that for someone else too.

Thank you to the AI tools that helped me build along the way. You made parts of this faster, clearer, and more possible. You were also wrong sometimes in ways that forced me to become a better builder, which, honestly, was part of the gift too.

And most of all, thank you to my daughter, Ava.

You listened to me talk about MemeScanr before it was a product, before it was an app, before it was even fully an idea. You were there while I was still figuring it out. You became my first user, first beta tester, and first bug reporter before MemeScanr was even real.

This book is for many people, but in a special way, it is also for you.

You are one of the biggest reasons I kept going.
