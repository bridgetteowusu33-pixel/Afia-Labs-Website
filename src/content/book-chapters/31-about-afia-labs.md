---Afia Labs is a small software studio building consumer tools for people who want their phones to feel more like their own.

Our first product is MemeScanr, a photo cleanup app for iPhone that turns a chore into something you actually enjoy doing.

We operate on three principles:

1. **On-device by default.** Your data stays on your device unless there's a compelling, user-visible reason to leave it.
2. **Voice as design.** How our products talk to you is as much a product decision as what they do.
3. **Ownership-first.** We build for people who want control over their tools, not people who want their tools to control them.

You can find us at [afialabs.com](https://afialabs.com), and MemeScanr at [memescanr.com](https://memescanr.com).
