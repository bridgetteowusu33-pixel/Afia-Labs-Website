Afia Labs is the name I build under.

It started as a container for the kinds of things I wanted to make real: apps, tools, ideas, and now this book too. What matters to me is not just building software that works. It is building products that feel useful, human, and full of personality.

Right now, that includes MemeScanr, a playful iPhone photo cleanup app that makes storage cleanup feel lighter and smarter. It includes Planet Wonders, a kids app built to make learning feel magical, creative, and full of discovery. It also includes TripSquad, a group travel planning idea built around the real challenge of getting people to actually agree.

The products are different, but the foundation is the same.

I care about ownership. I care about personality. I care about usefulness that still feels good to use.

I want the things I build to help people feel more in control of their digital lives, not less. I want them to feel like someone thought about the emotional experience, not just the technical one. And I want them to feel alive. Not cold. Not generic. Not like they were built without care.

That is what Afia Labs is for.

A place to build products with heart, clarity, and real intention.

You can find us at afialabs.net.
