Afia Labs is a software studio building thoughtful consumer apps for modern life.

We create products that are useful, human, and designed with personality, apps that help people organize, explore, create, and experience everyday life in better ways. Our work spans utility, education, and lifestyle, with each product built around the same core belief: technology should feel empowering, personal, and genuinely enjoyable to use.

Our products and active projects include MemeScanr, a playful iPhone photo cleanup app that makes storage cleanup feel lighter and smarter; Planet Wonders, a kid-focused learning experience built to make discovery and creativity feel magical; and TripSquad, a group travel planning concept designed around the real challenge of getting people to actually agree.

Even though the products are different, they are all built from the same foundation.

We operate on three principles:

Ownership-first.
We build for people who want more control over their digital lives, not less. Our products should help people feel more in charge of their tools, decisions, and experiences.

Personality matters.
We believe software should not feel cold. How a product talks, guides, and interacts with people is part of the product itself. Voice is not decoration. It is design.

Usefulness should still feel good.
We want practical products to feel thoughtful, polished, and alive. An app can solve a real problem and still feel warm, fun, or memorable.

You can find us at afialabs.net.
