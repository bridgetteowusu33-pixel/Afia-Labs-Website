## Why This Should Be a Bundle and Not Just a Book

This idea is bigger than one format.

The book gives people belief, language, strategy, and identity.
The workbook gives them structure, prompts, reflection space, and implementation tools.
The course gives them guided execution, momentum, and a more premium transformation.

Together, these three formats create something stronger than any single one on its own.

The book says, *this is possible and here is the philosophy.*
The workbook says, *now sit down and do the work.*
The course says, *let me walk you through it step by step.*

That is a real ecosystem.

And it fits Afia Labs beautifully because Afia Labs does not have to be only an app brand. It can also become a builder brand — a place where people learn how to create things they actually own.

## 2. The Workbook

**Recommended title:** The AI App Builder Workbook

**Subtitle:** Your Afia Labs Action Guide from Idea to Launch

### What the Workbook Does

The workbook turns passive reading into active progress. It helps the student stop consuming and start making decisions. It gives them places to write, clarify, validate, choose, test, and plan.

### Purpose of the Workbook in the Bundle

- Helps implementation
- Increases perceived value of the bundle
- Supports different learning styles
- Gives the student something tangible to "work through"
- Makes the course easier to follow
- Increases completion and results

### Tone of the Workbook

- Encouraging
- Clear
- Uncluttered
- Direct
- Practical
- Slightly coach-like

### Workbook Sections

**Section 1: Builder Identity**
- What kind of builder do I want to become?
- Where am I currently acting like a passenger?
- Where do I need more ownership?
- What is my reason for building?

**Section 2: Idea Clarity**
- What problem do I want to solve?
- Who is the user?
- What pain point feels real enough to build around?
- What does success look like for the user?

**Section 3: Idea Filter**
- Are people already paying for this?
- Can I reach the audience?
- Is the problem painful, repetitive, expensive, or urgent?
- Can one person realistically build and maintain this with AI help?

**Section 4: MVP Planning**
- What are the 3 must-have features?
- What is version 2, not version 1?
- What does the user journey look like?
- What is the smallest useful version of this app?

**Section 5: Prompt Builder Pages**
- Messy idea dump page
- Clean prompt conversion page
- VS Code coding prompt worksheet
- Debugging prompt worksheet
- Polish/refinement prompt worksheet

**Section 6: Stack & Setup**
- My chosen stack
- Editor choice
- AI tool choice
- Backend choice
- Payment choice
- Deployment choice
- Why I chose this stack

**Section 7: Testing & Launch Prep**
- Simulator checklist
- Real device checklist
- TestFlight checklist
- App Store / Play Store prep sheet
- Trust/safety checks
- Pricing checks

**Section 8: Business Setup**
- Business name brainstorm
- Domain search tracker
- Trademark/basic conflict check notes
- LLC setup tracker
- Website pages checklist
- Business email setup tracker

**Section 9: Marketing Plan**
- Audience definition
- Social content themes
- Launch post ideas
- Brand voice words
- Offer positioning notes
- Launch week checklist

**Section 10: Monetization & Next Steps**
- Pricing decision page
- Revenue model page
- What I will track after launch
- What I learned from version 1
- What I want to build next

### Workbook Bonuses

- Printable planning sheets
- Weekly builder check-in pages
- AI-free brainstorm pages
- Launch confidence checklist
- Post-launch reflection pages

## 5. Customer Journey Under Afia Labs

A smart customer journey could look like this:

1. Someone discovers you through social media, a post, a reel, a thread, or your website.
2. They resonate with the message: build with AI, own the outcome.
3. They buy the book or join the email list.
4. They realize they want more structure and buy the workbook.
5. They want guided execution and upgrade into the course.
6. Later, they buy future Afia Labs templates, resources, or products.

That is how the bundle becomes more than one sale. It becomes a product ecosystem.

## 7. Visual / Brand Direction for the Bundle

### Style Direction

- Clean
- Premium but warm
- Modern
- Optimistic
- Smart
- Grounded
- Feminine but not overly soft if that matches your preference
- Digitally savvy

### Possible Color Direction

Use your Afia Labs brand colors, then create subtle variations for each product:

- **Book** = core brand color
- **Workbook** = lighter / more practical planning version
- **Course** = bolder premium version

### Visual System Idea

Make the three products feel like one family:
- Same typography family
- Same icon style
- Same layout system
- Different accent color or subtitle line for each product

That makes the bundle feel polished and intentional.

## 8. Launch Strategy for the Bundle

### Soft Launch Sequence

**Phase 1:** Reveal the message and philosophy publicly: *Build with AI. Own the outcome.*

**Phase 2:** Launch the book first as the trust-builder.

**Phase 3:** Release the workbook as the action companion.

**Phase 4:** Open the course as the premium implementation offer.

This staggered release can help the brand feel more alive and gives you more marketing moments.

### Alternative

Launch them together as one ecosystem, but still present the course as the premium flagship.
