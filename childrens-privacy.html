Children's Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

Afia Labs is committed to protecting the privacy and safety of children who use our applications, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Apps Designed for Children

Planet Wonders is designed for children ages 5–10 and is built with child safety as a priority.

Information Collection

Afia Labs does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected from children
  • No account creation is required
  • No names, email addresses, or phone numbers are collected
  • No public sharing features are available

Parental Rights

Parents or guardians may:

  • Review any information collected (none is currently collected)
  • Request deletion of any data

To exercise these rights, contact:

support@afialabs.net

Safety Measures

Afia Labs takes the following steps to protect children's privacy:

  • Minimizing data collection
  • Avoiding behavioral tracking
  • Designing age-appropriate experiences

Contact

If you have questions about children's privacy:

support@afialabs.net
