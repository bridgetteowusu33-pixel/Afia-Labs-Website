Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026

Afia Labs is committed to protecting user privacy and providing transparency about how data is handled and deleted.

User Data Deletion

Most Afia Labs applications, including MemeScanr and Planet Wonders, do not require user accounts and do not store personal user data on Afia Labs servers.

Because of this:

  • Most data remains stored locally on the user's device
  • Deleting the app removes all locally stored app data

Requesting Data Deletion

If you have contacted Afia Labs through email or support and would like your information deleted, you may request deletion by contacting:

support@afialabs.net

We will delete the requested data within a reasonable timeframe unless we are legally required to retain it.

App Permissions

Users may revoke app permissions at any time through their device settings, including:

  • Photo library access
  • Notifications
  • Other device permissions

Contact

If you have questions about data deletion:

support@afialabs.net
